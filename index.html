The Well of Rummah

12/11/2025 2:30pm

The Legacy of Selfless Giving

During a severe drought in Medina, the only source of fresh water was a well owned by a man who charged heavily for it. Uthman ibn Affan (ra), seeing the suffering of the people, purchased the well with his own wealth and dedicated it for the free use of all Muslims. This act of "Sadaqah Jariyah" continues to provide blessings even today, teaching us that true wealth increases only when it is shared with those in need...

Our Mission & Vision

Assalamu Alaikum. 'Imaner Jhilik' is a humble initiative dedicated to spreading the beautiful and timeless teachings of Islam in an easy and accessible way. In today’s fast-paced world, we believe that authentic spiritual knowledge is the key to a peaceful and meaningful life.

Through our content, we strive to share inspiring stories of the Prophets, the exemplary honesty of the Sahabas, and practical Islamic reminders for our daily lives. Alhamdulillah, we are now a growing family of over 535 dedicated members. Our goal is not fame, but to seek the pleasure of Allah (SWT) and to ignite the light of Iman in every heart. We sincerely thank you for being a part of our journey.
